Your guide to understanding blood donation and transfusion

Blood donations are crucial for patients needing transfusions due to surgery, injury, or illness. They save lives and support those with chronic conditions.

  • Whole Blood Donation: A pint of blood is collected.
  • Platelet Donation: Platelets are separated, often used for cancer patients.
  • Plasma Donation: Plasma is collected for clotting factors.

One can donate whole blood every 56 days. Platelets can be donated more frequently (check with your center).
