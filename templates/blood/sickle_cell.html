How Blood Donation Helps Sickle Cell Patients

Sickle cell disease causes red blood cells to become misshapen and sticky, blocking blood flow and causing severe pain, anemia, and organ damage.

Your blood donation provides healthy red blood cells that:

  • Improve oxygen delivery throughout the body
  • Reduce painful vaso-occlusive crises
  • Lower the risk of stroke and acute chest syndrome
  • Prevent severe anemia and organ damage
  • Enhance quality of life and longevity
